Backbone Maps

What is a backbone map?

A backbone map is our term for the map that you have selected to serve as the primary map onto which all other maps are aligned. For this reason, you are only able to load a single chromosome from the backbone map at one time.

You can load another backbone map at any time, but this operation will replace anything that you currently have loaded into memory. Alternatively, consider opening a new window and loading a new backbone map into the other window.

In order to open a new window simply:

  • Click on "File...New Window" from the menubar
  • Press Ctrl+n after selecting the main VCMap window
